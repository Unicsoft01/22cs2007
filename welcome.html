The proposed web-based Mobile Phone Swapping and Trading System aims to provide a robust platform for users to exchange mobile phones seamlessly. Users begin by signing up and creating detailed profiles, listing phones they wish to swap with specifications and conditions. They can search for listings based on preferences, engage in negotiations through an in-app messaging system, and finalize terms through a digital trade agreement. Physical inspection of phones is arranged before finalizing the trade. Post-trade, users can rate and provide feedback, contributing to a trustworthy community. Updating profiles after trades ensures transparency and credibility within the platform, fostering a reliable environment for mobile phone exchanges.
